Ung vien de xuat chia khu vuc thanh 3 cum van hanh va bo tri Area Supervisor cho moi cum.
Muc tieu trong 90 ngay la giam 15 phan tram overtime, tang ti le tuan thu checklist len 95 phan tram va giam khiem nai lap lai 20 phan tram.

Phan tich hien trang:
- Lich lam viec chua can bang, co cua hang thieu nguoi vao khung gio cao diem.
- Quan ly cua hang chua theo sat KPI ngay.
- Quy trinh ban giao ca va checklist mo cua, dong cua khong dong nhat.

Giai phap:
- Chuan hoa dashboard ngay cho tung cua hang, review 2 lan moi ngay.
- Dao tao Store Manager ve cach lap lich va quan ly nang suat.
- Ap dung kiem tra cheo giua cac cua hang trong cung cum.
- Xep hang uu tien cua hang theo muc do anh huong doanh thu va rui ro van hanh.

Ke hoach trien khai:
- Tuan 1-2: danh gia du lieu, xac dinh top 10 cua hang can can thiep.
- Tuan 3-6: pilot o 10 cua hang.
- Tuan 7-12: nhan rong toan khu vuc neu KPI cai thien.

KPI:
- Overtime
- Checklist compliance
- Complaint repeat rate
- Sales per labor hour

Rui ro:
- Quan ly cua hang phan khang khi tang tan suat kiem tra.
- Du lieu cham cong chua sach.

Xu ly:
- Truyen thong ro muc tieu va co thuong theo KPI.
- Audit du lieu 2 tuan dau.
