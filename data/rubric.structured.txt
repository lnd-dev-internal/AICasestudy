BAREM CHAM DIEM CASE STUDY AREA MANAGER

Tong diem toi da: 100

NGUYEN TAC CHAM CHUNG
- Cham diem theo tung muc ben duoi, khong cong diem cho y khong lien quan.
- Uu tien bai lam co su dung du lieu, so lieu, bang chung cu the tu dataset/de bai.
- Neu ung vien co lap luan hop ly, co data chung minh nhung khac dap an mau, van co the cho diem mot phan hoac toi da neu logic vung.
- Chap nhan sai so ±2% o phan tinh KPI neu phuong phap tinh hop ly.
- Khong cho diem vuot muc toi da cua moi muc.
- Khi cho diem tung muc, can neu bang chung cu the tu bai lam.

PHAN 1. PHAN TICH HIEN TRANG - 30 DIEM

1.1. Tinh toan va danh gia KPI - 12 diem

1.1.A. Tinh toan chinh xac - 6 diem
- Muc 1: Tinh dung KPI trung binh Buu cuc A - toi da 2 diem
  Dieu kien dat:
  - GTC tong xap xi 80-81%
  - GTC TTS xap xi 84-85%
  - FD xap xi 1.0-1.1%
- Muc 2: Tinh dung KPI trung binh Buu cuc B - toi da 2 diem
  Dieu kien dat:
  - GTC tong xap xi 51-52%
  - GTC TTS xap xi 53-54%
  - FD xap xi 1.9-2.1%
- Muc 3: Tinh dung KPI trung binh Khu vuc - toi da 2 diem
  Dieu kien dat:
  - Co tinh trung binh co trong so hoac tong hop hop ly

1.1.B. So sanh va danh gia voi muc tieu - 6 diem
- Muc 1: So sanh tung KPI voi muc tieu - 2 diem
  Dieu kien dat:
  - Co bang hoac noi dung so sanh ro rang tung chi so voi target
- Muc 2: Xac dinh dung KPI dat/khong dat - 2 diem
  Dieu kien dat:
  - Buu cuc A: dat GTC tong, dat GTC TTS, dat FD
  - Buu cuc B: khong dat ca 3 KPI
- Muc 3: Danh gia muc do chenh lech - 2 diem
  Dieu kien dat:
  - Co neu cu the gap, vi du B thap hon target khoang 28-30% GTC

1.2. Xac dinh van de va nguyen nhan goc re - 12 diem

1.2.A. Xac dinh van de chinh - 4 diem
- Van de 1: % Gan don Buu cuc B thap, xap xi 74% so voi 99% cua A - 1 diem
  Ghi chu: insight quan trong nhat
- Van de 2: % GTC Buu cuc B thap, thap hon A khoang 30% - 1 diem
- Van de 3: Leadtime Buu cuc B cao, gap khoang 2 lan A - 1 diem
- Van de 4: Nhan su Buu cuc B bat on, co 4 NV co y dinh nghi - 1 diem

1.2.B. Phan tich nguyen nhan goc re co su dung data - 4 diem
- Muc 1: Chi ra moi lien he % Gan thap -> GTC thap - 1 diem
  Dieu kien dat:
  - Co dan chung so lieu cu the
- Muc 2: Chi ra moi lien he thieu nhan su -> khong xu ly het don - 1 diem
  Dieu kien dat:
  - Co tinh nang luc xu ly so voi don can giao
- Muc 3: Phat hien pattern theo ngay, Chu Nhat va dau tuan thap hon - 1 diem
  Dieu kien dat:
  - Co so sanh data theo thu
- Muc 4: Phat hien van de thu nhap thap -> NV muon nghi - 1 diem
  Dieu kien dat:
  - Co lien he thu nhap trung binh voi ghi chu nghi viec

1.2.C. Logic giai thich - 4 diem
- Chuoi logic nhan qua ro rang - toi da 2 diem
  Vi du:
  - Thieu NV -> Khong gan het don -> Ton don -> Leadtime tang -> FD tang
- Co so do hoac mo ta moi quan he giua cac yeu to - toi da 2 diem
  Dieu kien dat:
  - The hien duoc tu duy he thong

Huong dan cham muc 1.2.C:
- 4 diem: chuoi logic hoan chinh, co diagram/mo ta ro rang
- 2-3 diem: co logic nhung chua day du hoac thieu dan chung
- 0-1 diem: logic roi rac, khong thay moi lien he

1.3. Xac dinh buu cuc uu tien - 6 diem
- Ket luan dung: Buu cuc B can uu tien - 2 diem
- Ly do dua tren data van hanh - 2 diem
  Dieu kien dat:
  - Dan chung KPI gap, % gan, leadtime
- Ly do dua tren data nhan su - 2 diem
  Dieu kien dat:
  - Dan chung ty le NV muon nghi, nang suat

Luu y phan 1.3:
- Neu ung vien chon Buu cuc A nhung lap luan hop ly va co data chung minh, van co the cho diem phan lap luan toi da 4/6.

PHAN 2. RA QUYET DINH VA XU LY VAN DE - 50 DIEM

2.1. Chon van de uu tien - 15 diem

2.1.A. Xac dinh van de uu tien - 5 diem
- Xuat sac - 5 diem
  Dieu kien dat:
  - Chon van de cot loi: Nhan su B hoac % Gan B
  - Day la nguyen nhan goc
- Tot - 3 den 4 diem
  Dieu kien dat:
  - Chon van de quan trong nhung la he qua, vi du GTC thap hoac Leadtime cao
- Trung binh - 1 den 2 diem
  Dieu kien dat:
  - Chon van de dung nhung khong phai uu tien cao nhat
- Yeu - 0 diem
  Dieu kien dat:
  - Chon sai hoac khong chon duoc

Danh sach dap an uu tien cao:
- On dinh nhan su Buu cuc B, bao gom giu chan 4 NV co y dinh nghi va tuyen bo sung
- Tang % Gan don Buu cuc B
- Tang nang luc xu ly don Buu cuc B

2.1.B. Giai thich ly do uu tien theo data - 10 diem
- Dan chung data cu the - 4 diem
  Vi du:
  - 4/20 NV = 20% muon nghi
- Giai thich tac dong domino - 3 diem
  Dieu kien dat:
  - Neu khong giai quyet thi hau qua gi, co logic
- So sanh voi cac van de khac - 3 diem
  Dieu kien dat:
  - Neu ro vi sao van de nay quan trong hon cac van de con lai

2.2. De xuat 3 hanh dong cu the - 35 diem

2.2.A. Hanh dong 1 - 12 diem
- Muc tieu ro rang - 3 diem
- Can cu du lieu - 4 diem
- Ket qua ky vong hop ly - 3 diem
- Kha thi voi vai tro Area Manager - 2 diem

2.2.B. Hanh dong 2 - 12 diem
- Muc tieu ro rang - 3 diem
- Can cu du lieu - 4 diem
- Ket qua ky vong hop ly - 3 diem
- Kha thi voi vai tro Area Manager - 2 diem

2.2.C. Hanh dong 3 - 11 diem
- Muc tieu ro rang - 3 diem
- Can cu du lieu - 3 diem
- Ket qua ky vong hop ly - 3 diem
- Kha thi voi vai tro Area Manager - 2 diem

Huong dan cham cho moi hanh dong:
- Muc tieu ro rang:
  - Muc tieu phai cu the, do luong duoc
- Can cu du lieu:
  - Phai dan chung data cu the lam co so quyet dinh
- Ket qua ky vong hop ly:
  - Realistic, co the dat trong tuan dau hoac giai doan ngan
- Kha thi voi vai tro Area Manager:
  - Nam trong quyen han va nguon luc thuc te

Hanh dong mau duoc danh gia cao:
- Gap rieng 4 NV co y dinh nghi de tim hieu va dua giai phap giu chan
  Ly do tot:
  - Giai quyet nguyen nhan goc, data-driven
- Dieu phoi NV tu Buu cuc A sang ho tro B vao ngay cao diem
  Ly do tot:
  - Kha thi, dua tren data nang luc xu ly
- Ra soat va toi uu route/tuyen giao de tang % Gan
  Ly do tot:
  - Giai quyet truc tiep van de % Gan thap
- Dao tao kem cap NV moi cua B, nhom co 5 NV duoi 3 thang
  Ly do tot:
  - Data-driven, tang nang suat
- De xuat tuyen bo sung 2-3 NV cho Buu cuc B
  Ly do tot:
  - Giai quyet thieu hut nang luc

Hanh dong bi tru diem:
- "Hop dong vien nhan vien" ma khong co plan cu the
  Ly do tru:
  - Chung chung, khong data-driven
- "Tang cuong giam sat" ma khong neu giam sat gi
  Ly do tru:
  - Khong ro rang
- Hanh dong nam ngoai quyen han Area Manager
  Ly do tru:
  - Khong kha thi
- Hanh dong khong lien quan den van de da xac dinh
  Ly do tru:
  - Khong logic

PHAN 3. LAP KE HOACH CAI THIEN - 20 DIEM

3.1. Muc tieu tung tuan - 8 diem
- Co muc tieu so cu the cho tung tuan - 4 diem
  Vi du:
  - Tuan 1: % Gan dat 80%
  - Tuan 2: dat 85%
- Muc tieu co tinh ke thua, tang dan hop ly - 2 diem
  Dieu kien dat:
  - Khong nhay vot phi thuc te
- Muc tieu bam sat KPI yeu cau - 2 diem
  Dieu kien dat:
  - Huong den target GTC 80%, FD 1.3%

Huong dan cham muc 3.1:
- 7-8 diem: day du 4 tuan, moi tuan co 2-3 muc tieu so cu the
- 4-6 diem: co muc tieu nhung chua du cu the hoac thieu tuan
- 1-3 diem: muc tieu chung chung, kho do luong
- 0 diem: khong co muc tieu hoac muc tieu phi thuc te

3.2. Cac hanh dong chinh - 8 diem
- Hanh dong cu the, chi tiet cho tung tuan - 3 diem
  Dieu kien dat:
  - Ai lam, lam gi, khi nao
- Hanh dong lien ket voi muc tieu tuong ung - 2 diem
- Hanh dong kha thi voi nguon luc hien co - 2 diem
  Dieu kien dat:
  - Dua tren so NV, dac thu Buu cuc
- Hanh dong co tinh sang tao hoac phu hop thuc te GHN - 1 diem

3.3. Cach theo doi va danh gia - 4 diem
- Co chi so do luong ro rang - 2 diem
  Vi du:
  - % GTC hang ngay, % Gan, so don ton
- Co tan suat va cach thuc theo doi - 1 diem
  Vi du:
  - Review daily/weekly, dashboard
- Co co che dieu chinh neu khong dat - 1 diem
  Vi du:
  - Co plan B neu muc tieu khong dat
